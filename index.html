Introduction

      Brain dysfunction ranges from complete loss of consciousness to disorientation and an inability to pay attention. The interventions focusing on defining the extent of brain damage and its precise location and exploring personalized neuromodulation technologies are of prime importance. Combining engineering with medical biological sciences to promote health care treatment, including diagnosis, monitoring, and therapy has grown exponentially in recent years. Such as the develop on the neuroimaging technologies and the use of invasive and especially non-invasive neuromodulation.

The Theme of this FORUM

      The main theme of the 3rd International forum on brain dysfunction and neuromodulation (IFBDN) --bridging the gap between doctors and bioengineers, is to meet clinical needs with advanced engineering technologies establishing the integrity brain network and neuro-modulating for brain dysfunction.

The photos of last year (above and right)

The GOAL of IFBDN

       IFBDN seeks to strengthen international academic exchange, inter- disciplinary communications and cooperation and aims to foster inspirational dialogues among the world’s leading basic science and clinical researchers. The integrity connection of brain network will display how the brain is impacted in a number of brain disorders, including Stroke, Alzheimer’s disease, Parkinson’s disease, Huntington’s disease, Depression, Anxiety, and damage due to injury to specific brain regions. The combination of brain dysfunction and engineering evolves gradually over the developmental course and indicates a potential therapeutic window during which pathophysiological processes may be modified to halt disease progression or reduce its severity.
